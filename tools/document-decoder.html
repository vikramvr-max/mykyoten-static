Document Decoder: Scan Japanese Documents in English

Scan any Japanese document and get a plain English explanation of what it means, what action is needed, and whether it affects your visa.

The MyKyoten Document Decoder scans Japanese-language documents and extracts deadlines, amounts, and action items in plain English.

Upload a photo of any Japanese document. The decoder identifies the document type, extracts key fields including due dates and payment amounts, and tells you what action is required and whether it affects your visa status or PR eligibility.

Documents the decoder handles include National Health Insurance notices, resident tax bills, pension payment slips, annual tax withholding slips, utility bills, and official letters from the ward office or immigration bureau.

For compliance-critical documents the decoder explains the consequence of missing the deadline in plain English. It connects scanned documents to your visa compliance record and PR readiness tracker automatically.

No Japanese reading ability required. Scan the document, read the English summary, and know exactly what to do next.
