Moving to Japan: Step-by-Step Checklist

Complete step-by-step checklist for moving to Japan. What to do before you leave, at the airport, and in your first 30 days.

Japan expects you to do things in a very specific sequence - doing them out of order wastes time.

Before arrival, focus only on visa eligibility and timing - you do not need a permanent address or bank account yet.

You have 14 days from arrival to register your address at city hall.

Address registration unlocks other systems like health insurance, My Number, and banking.

Understanding what can wait helps you prioritize mandatory steps and reduce panic.
