Do You Actually Need to Change Your Status, or Just Renew It?

This is where people mess up before they even start. A **change of status of residence** (在留資格変更許可申請) is required when you are moving from one visa category to a fundamentally different one. Common triggers: - You were on a Student visa and are now starting full-time employment - You married a Japanese national and want to switch from a work visa to a Spouse of Japanese National visa - You changed jobs into a role that falls under a different visa category than what you currently hold (for example, switching from Engineer/Specialist in Humanities/International Services to Instructor) A change of status is **not** required if you are simply renewing within the same category, even if you change employers, as long as the work type matches your existing status.
