Missing a Payment Deadline: This Is Where People Mess Up

Japan's late payment penalty on resident tax is not trivial. The current statutory rate runs at **8.7% per year for the first two months** past the due date, escalating to **14.6% per year** thereafter, as set by Japanese tax law. On a ¥38,000 instalment, that is roughly ¥277 per month at the base rate, climbing steeply if months pass.
