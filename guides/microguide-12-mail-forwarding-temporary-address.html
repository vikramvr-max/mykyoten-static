Mail Forwarding Japan: What to Do When You Are Still in a Hotel or Airbnb

Living in temporary housing in Japan? Here is how Japan Post forwarding works, what ID you need, and how to avoid missing important government mail during your first weeks.

Japan Post will forward your mail from a temporary address to your permanent one, but you need to register the forwarding service before you move-and the process assumes you have proof of residence at both addresses. The workaround: Use the Tenzai Todoke (転居届) form at any post office with your Residence Card (在留カード) showing your temporary address, then update it again when you move to your real place.
