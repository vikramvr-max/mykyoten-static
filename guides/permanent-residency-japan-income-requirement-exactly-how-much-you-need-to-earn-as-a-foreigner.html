How Immigration Reviews Your 5-Year or 10-Year Income Track Record

The standard permanent residency path requires 10 years of residence with 5 consecutive years on an eligible work or spousal visa. The point system route can reduce this to 3 years or 1 year, but income scrutiny intensifies with shorter tracks. Officers will request tax payment certificates (nouzei shomeisho) or residence tax certificates (kazei shomeisho) for **the past 3 to 5 years minimum**.
