What checkboxes should I select when requesting a juminhyo?

For employment, select My Number and nationality and visa status. For bank accounts and apartment applications, select nationality and visa status. For immigration purposes, select nationality and visa status plus previous address. The basic version without extras is rarely sufficient for official purposes.
