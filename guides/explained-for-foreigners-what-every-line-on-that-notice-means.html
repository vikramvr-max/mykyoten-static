How to Read Your Payslip and Confirm Your Employer Is Actually Paying

Your monthly resident tax deduction appears on your payslip (給与明細書) under the deductions column (控除). The label is typically **住民税** or **市民税・県民税**. The amount should match the monthly installment (月割額) on your resident tax notice exactly. Here is the part nobody tells you: your employer collects this money from your salary and is legally required to remit it to the municipality by the 10th of the following month (or January 10 for the December deduction). You have no way to confirm this happened from your payslip alone. What you can do is request a **特別徴収税額の納付確認** (confirmation of remittance) from your city hall by providing your name and address. Some municipalities allow this inquiry at the counter; others require a written request.
