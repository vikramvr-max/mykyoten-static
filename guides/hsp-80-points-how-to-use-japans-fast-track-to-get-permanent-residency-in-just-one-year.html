Japan's Highly Skilled Professional (HSP) visa uses a point-based system managed by the Ministry of Justice. That one-year window is real, and it is genuinely faster than any other PR pathway in Japan. Points are scored across five categories: academic background, professional career, annual salary, age, and bonus factors. Each category has defined scores. A doctoral degree adds 30 points. What Documents Does HSP Permanent Residency Actually Require?

The HSP PR document package is not the same as a standard PR checklist. The standard PR route leans heavily on tax payment history and years of residence.
